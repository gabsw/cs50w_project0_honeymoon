Relaxation

I only really and truly fully relax on my own. Give me a sun lounger, a pool and a sea view, and I'm happy.

Miranda Hart

The beauty of a beach honeymoon is that there are really no demands on you. This trip will be a chance to sleep late, snooze in the sun and drift into the ocean. It's about enjoying life's little pleasures: feeling the sand between your toes, the smell of sun cream and the sound of waves lapping.
