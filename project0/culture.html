Culture

A people without the knowledge of their past history, origin and culture is like a tree without roots.

Marcus Garvey

To avoid feeling a sense of climax after the wedding, you'll want a honeymoon that keeps you busy, interested and entertained. You will want to explore a new place, experience an alternative culture and learn about a very different way of life. The key to grow within your relationship and challenge yourselves is to delve deep into your sense of curiosity.
